SlowRep Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

SlowRep ("the App") is a workout tracking application provided by Daisuke Kitagawa ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal information.

All workout data recorded in the App (exercises, sets, reps, weight, timer records, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share any user data with third parties.

Data Export

The App includes a feature to export workout data in JSON format. This feature is only executed when the user voluntarily initiates it, and the management of exported data is the user's responsibility.

Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use any analytics tools or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be notified on this page.
